Grounds

Cricket is a popular sport in Sri Lanka.There are eight grounds that have been used to host international cricket matches and seven of them have hosted Test Matches.However the Colombo District club ground and the Tyronne Fernando stadium are no longer used for matches at International level.

Galle Stadium

This is surrounded by an amazing beautiful environmet by Indian Ocean.This is owned by Galle Cricket club.In 1876 this was establshed and also identified as luckiest venues for the Sri Lankan Cricket team.

Pallekele Stadium

This is situated in Kandy,Sri Lanka.This was established in 2009.The first ODI was held in this.The first test match was between Sri Lanka and west Indies was playedfrom 1 December to 5 December.

Premadasa Stadium

This stadium was known as Keththarama stadium before 1994.More than 100 one day matches were held in here.This is the place where the world record for the highest test total declared by Sri Lanka.
